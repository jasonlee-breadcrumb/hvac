Common Questions

How quickly can you get to my home? expand_more
For AC and heating emergencies, we typically have a technician on-site in Newbury Park or Thousand Oaks within 2-4 hours. We offer 24/7 dispatching.
Do you offer financing for new systems? expand_more
Yes! We offer flexible financing options with low monthly payments for all new system installations. Ask your technician about our current HERO and PACE programs.
What brands do you service? expand_more
We are factory-authorized dealers for major brands but our technicians are NATE-certified to repair all makes and models including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, and Rheem.
Are your technicians licensed? expand_more
Absolutely. All Southland technicians are NATE-certified, fully licensed, bonded, and insured in the state of California. We background-check every team member.
